About US

Piston & Rings Enterprises is a Sole Proprietorship business founded in 1997 by Edward M. Enriquez. Started out in a garage with only one forklift to offer. Through the core values shown by the owner, the company is still growing and gaining more and more equipment each year, moved to its own shop offering a lot more space for equipment storage, and become a successful business operating throughout CALABARZON and NCR.
